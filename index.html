Welcome home

A community gathered by living water

Sacred Waters Ministry is a faith community rooted in worship, fellowship, and the spiritual heritage of our people. Wherever you are, you have a place at the table with us.

We gather each week in spirit and truth — lifting prayer, sharing the Word, and walking together in faith. Come as you are; leave renewed.
